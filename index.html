Onio

Private Screenshot & Screen Recording App

Effective Date: February 2026

Onio ("the App") is a private screenshot and screen recording application. This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles your data.

1. Data We Collect

The App captures the following data solely at your explicit request:

  • Screenshots of your screen
  • Screen recordings (video)
  • System audio during screen recordings (if audio permission is granted)
  • Photos taken with your device camera

All captured content is stored locally on your device. No data is transmitted to any server or third party.

2. How We Use Your Data

Captured screenshots, recordings, and photos are stored in the App's private storage on your device and are only accessible within Onio. If you enable "Save to Gallery", copies are saved to your device's public Photos app in an "Onio" folder. Exported copies are not encrypted and are accessible to other apps on your device.

3. Permissions We Use

  • Display over other apps (SYSTEM_ALERT_WINDOW): Used to display the floating capture button that lets you take screenshots or stop recordings from any screen.
  • Screen capture (MediaProjection): Used exclusively to capture screenshots and record your screen. Requires your explicit consent each session.
  • Microphone (RECORD_AUDIO): Used to capture system audio playback during screen recordings. Only active while recording is in progress.
  • Camera (CAMERA): Used to take photos through the built-in camera feature.
  • Post Notifications: Used to display the persistent foreground service notification required by Android while screenshot or recording services are active.
  • Biometric (USE_BIOMETRIC): Used for optional fingerprint authentication to unlock the App.

4. Data Storage & Security

All captured content is stored locally on your device in the App's private directory. Optional AES-256 encryption (via Android Keystore) can be enabled in Settings. System backups are disabled for this App to prevent accidental cloud upload of your private captures.

5. Data Sharing

Onio does not share, sell, or transmit any captured data to third parties. Your screenshots, recordings, and photos never leave your device unless you explicitly share or export them yourself.

6. Data Retention & Deletion

You control all data. You can delete individual screenshots or recordings at any time within the Gallery. You can also enable auto-delete in Settings to automatically remove captures older than a specified number of days. Uninstalling the App permanently deletes all privately stored data.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be reflected in the App's Play Store listing and within the App itself.

9. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at onioappshelp@outlook.com.
